Chief Executive Officer - SRQ Robotics 2019 - present

SRQ Robotics is an engineering firm providing software and electronic hardware related services. We have clients from 20+ countries due to the trusted and quality service that we provide. Our engineering team consists of various disciplines allowing us to cover different design requirements and perform quality assurance. We are engaged in, - IoT and Embedded System Development - PCB Designing and Prototyping - Full Stack and Mobile Application Development - Sensor Fusion, Digital Signal Processing and Simulations

Co-Founder - Qubebots 2022 - present

Qubebots introduces the newest X-Fly programmable nano drone to promote drone based STEM education in the world. X-Fly is capable of serving the entire spectrum of indoor programmable drone requirements with its hardware expandable modular design and comes with multi leveled development tool-kits to serve from school children to advanced researchers.

Professional Fellow 2023
